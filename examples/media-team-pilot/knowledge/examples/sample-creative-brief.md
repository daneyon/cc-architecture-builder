# Sample Creative Briefs

## Brief 1: "The Last Lighthouse Keeper"

> Create a short narrative video about the last lighthouse keeper in a world where all lighthouses have been automated. Follow their final night on duty — the routine, the memories, the quiet dignity of an ending era. Visual tone should be painterly and nostalgic with warm golden light. End with a sense of peaceful acceptance.

**Expected output**: ~90 second video, 8-10 scenes, contemplative narration

---

## Brief 2: "How Stars Are Born"

> Make an educational but poetic video explaining how stars form from nebulae. Start with the vast emptiness of space, show the gas clouds coming together, the ignition of fusion, and end with a newborn star lighting up its cosmic neighborhood. Make it feel awe-inspiring, not clinical.

**Expected output**: ~120 second video, 10-12 scenes, wonder-filled narration

---

## Brief 3: "A Day in the Life of a Raindrop"

> Tell the story of a single raindrop's journey from ocean evaporation to cloud formation to falling on a mountain and flowing through a river back to the sea. Whimsical, gentle tone. The raindrop should feel like a character on an adventure.

**Expected output**: ~60 second video, 6-8 scenes, playful narration
